About Me

Hello! I'm Jaideep Lingineni from Irving, Texas, and I’m a Computer Science student at Georgia Tech. I have a strong passion for understanding how things work, which inspired me to pursue a degree in Computer Science. This field allows me to explore the intricacies of computing and gain a deeper understanding of how computers operate.

Currently, I work as a Full Stack Software Engineer, where I apply my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React to develop dynamic and engaging web applications. I thrive on solving complex problems and creating efficient solutions that enhance user experiences.

Beyond my professional and academic pursuits, I enjoy participating in clubs such as Wreck Racing at Georgia Tech. Working on our team's race car combines my love for engineering with my passion for teamwork and innovation. In my free time, I enjoy cycling and working out, which keep me active and balanced.
