About Me

Hello! I'm Jaideep Lingineni, a Computer Science major at Georgia Tech with a deep passion for hardware, electronics, and programming. This fascination led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science at Georgia Tech, where I dive into the intricacies of programming, data structures, and algorithms. When I'm not immersed in my studies or coding, you'll often find me working on hardware projects. I love repairing broken electronics and breathing new life into them or creating something entirely new. Understanding how things work and making them better is a driving force in my life.
